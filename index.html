About

Kyrat is a fictional Himalayan country, based on Nepal and located in the Himalayas (bordering Bihar, India as stated by the nearest embassy being in Patna), that is featured as the setting in Far Cry 4. The Kingdom of Kyrat is an absolute monarchy, which in the game is ruled by the despotic self-appointed King Pagan Min.

Ancient times and origins:

It is believed in Kyrati mythology that Banashur created the world and his daughter, Kyra, created Kyrat. In 500 BC, Chal Jama Monastery was built by followers of Kyra, which also become the place where the teachings of Kyra were first recorded. Shanath arena was built in the 1750s by King Avinash to get citizens minds off issues. At this time, the arena featured elephants fighting tigers to the death.

Cities and Landmarks

Royal valley:

The royal valley is home to emperor Pagan-min himself the valley is surrounded by beautiful Himalayan mountains. Tourist location include Himalay trekking, flower garden, shangri-la temple and Jungle safari.

Utkarsh:

Utkarsh is located near the Himalay, the region witness snowfall every month, the also have unique animals Royal Tigers, Deer, Bear and many more. the city is known for its luxurious accomodation and hunting.

Banapur:

Banapur is very lively located in southern Kyrat. It consists of nine Official buildings, several shacks, a large ricefield, a shop, and a great collection of decorations and adornments. The city is also known for it's famous rice wine. which are aged between 13 months to 13 years. Gun range is a famous tourist attraction.

Tirtha:

Tirtha is sacred city known for it's Shrine and Monestary during annual festival. Monks around the world visit the famous Yuma shrine, To gain spirituality. The city offers holy book fairs, foreign goods, ornaments and many more.

Naccarapur:

Naccarapur is known as the city of cave, in 1979 the largest Diamond of Kyrat was found in tapas cave. Due to it's unique terrain the city attract many mountaineers, adventure seekers and hitch hikers. The mountain is blessing for old and sick it offer many herbal medicine. that is found only in this mountain range.

Jalendu:

Jalendu is a city of lakes the city have 8 unique lake, toga being at the highest altitude lake in the world. Many tourist end their journey in Jalendu. city offers spa, best cuisine and is favorite destination for your family and friends.
